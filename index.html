strengths and weaknesses

I consider my communication skills to be one of my greatest
strengths. Some people that dreams to be in the IT field assume
they don’t need strong communication skills to work in the tech
as communication skills are frequently underrated in terms of IT.
However, as what i have learned from my uncle that's a
programmer, such assumptions are false as most IT people are
supposed to communicate effectively and clearly with the
managers, clients, coworkers and even customers. In short,
technical skills are incomplete without soft skills like communication
skills. I am looking forward to hone and enhance this certain skill
in me.

As for my weaknesses, I find public speaking intimidating and have
often struggled with presentations. Shyness is also something that
I struggle in with groups. I find it intimidating to ask questions or
raise points, so I have often remained quiet in the past. Lastly,
self-criticism has brought me down a couple of times, i tend
to evaluate myself negatively most of the time. I am trying to be
more self-concious and aware of these weaknesses to try and
counter the negative effects of these such things.
